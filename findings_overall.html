Overall Physical Activity

Grade: D-**
202X Short-form Report (PDF) 202X Long-form Report (PDF)

Benchmark

% of children and adolescents who meet the Global Recommendations on Physical Activity for Health, which recommend that children and adolescents accumulate at least 60 min of moderate- to vigorous-intensity physical activity per day on average.

Key Findings

  • 89% of adolescents aged 11-18 years accumulated at least 60 min of MVPA per day (n = 552).
  • 22.1% of children in grades 1–3 had at least 60 minutes of daily MVPA over a week (n = 263).
  • 10% of children in primary 1–6 with mean age 9.3 years had at least 60 minutes of MVPA per day (n = 191).
